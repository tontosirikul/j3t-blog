About Me

I graduated from KMITL in 2022 with a BEng in Robotics and AI, then continued my studies at the University of Glasgow, earning a degree MSc in Software Development in 2023. Since 2024, I’ve been working as a software engineer at ExxonMobil.

I discovered my passion for software development during an internship at SCG, where I had the chance to work on factory production monitoring system as software developer and collaborate with experienced AI and IoT engineers. That experience sparked my curiosity and motivated me to pursue a master’s degree to deepen my understanding and expand my skills.

I’m currently interested in scalable backend systems, machine learning, and data engineering.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, playing video games, and building plastic model kits.
